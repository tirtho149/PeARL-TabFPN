                          WHAT'S BEING COMPARED
================================================================================

BASELINE (MLP Heads) vs FOLLOW-UP (TabPFN Heads)

BASELINE PEaRL:
  Vision Encoder (256-dim)
    ↓
  MLP Head for pathways:   Linear(256) → ReLU → Linear(200)
  MLP Head for genes:      Linear(256) → ReLU → Linear(1000)
  
  Training: MLP heads trained with SGD+MSE loss for 20 epochs
  Status: Baseline from paper (100% exact match)

FOLLOW-UP PEaRL:
  Vision Encoder (256-dim)  [SAME]
    ↓
  TabPFN Head for pathways: Pretrained model (32 estimators, fitted)
  TabPFN Head for genes:    Pretrained model (32 estimators, fitted)
  
  Training: TabPFN heads fitted on embeddings + targets (no SGD)
  Status: Novel follow-up study

================================================================================
                       TECHNICAL DETAILS
================================================================================

Architecture Changes:
  Stage 1: Completely identical (no changes)
           - Vision encoder: UNI ViT-L
           - Pathway encoder: Transformer + spatial encoding
           - Contrastive loss: Symmetric NT-Xent
  
  Stage 2: Different prediction heads
           Baseline:  MLP(256) with ReLU
           Follow-up: TabPFN (pretrained Bayesian model)

TabPFN Details:
  - Pretrained on millions of synthetic tabular datasets
  - Non-parametric Bayesian inference
  - Handles feature interactions automatically
  - sklearn-style fitting API
  - Natural uncertainty estimates

Training Procedure:
  Stage 1 (Both): 30 epochs SGD on contrastive loss
  Stage 2 Baseline: 20 epochs SGD on MSE loss
  Stage 2 Follow-up: Fit TabPFN on training embeddings

Hyperparameters (Shared):
  Learning rate: 1e-4
  Weight decay: 1e-3
  Batch size: 8
  Temperature: 0.07
  Embedding dim: 256
